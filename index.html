ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Rahul Garasiya, a passionate Business Analytics professional with an MBA in Information Technology from Chameli Devi Group of Institutions, Indore. My journey into the world of data began with a Bachelor's in Computer Applications from Idyllic Institute of Management, Indore, where I developed a keen interest in understanding how data can drive business decisions.

With a solid foundation in data analysis, visualization, and management, I have honed my skills in SQL, Excel, Power BI, and basic Python. I thrive on transforming complex data into actionable insights, leveraging strong report writing, data storytelling, and presentation abilities to communicate findings effectively. My experience in project management and excellent time management skills have enabled me to handle multiple tasks efficiently and deliver high-quality results.

Throughout my academic and professional journey, I have been committed to continuous learning and ethical considerations, ensuring that my work not only meets but exceeds expectations. I am a problem-solver at heart, with a knack for critical thinking and analytical reasoning. My ultimate goal is to leverage my skills to drive data-driven decisions and enhance operational efficiency in dynamic organizations.



Why I Am Interested in Business Analytics

My interest in business analytics started during my undergraduate studies when I was assigned a project to analyze social media data. Using Python, I conducted a sentiment analysis to gauge public opinion on various topics. The moment I presented my findings, showing how data could uncover hidden patterns and influence strategies, I knew I had found my passion. This experience ignited my enthusiasm for analytics and demonstrated the profound impact of data-driven decisions.

Further into my MBA program, I conducted a study on AI's impact on e-commerce businesses, which deepened my understanding of analytics' transformative power. This project solidified my commitment to helping organizations harness data's potential. Business analytics perfectly combines my love for numbers and problem-solving, allowing me to drive strategic decisions and improve business performance. I am eager to continue my journey in this field and contribute to the success of forward-thinking companies.
